About Me

Hi I Am Mario Xerri, a first year Masters Student at Stony Brook University studying Computer Science. I recently completed my undergraduate degree at Cornell University with a bachelors degree in computer science and a minor in applied math. My research interests include machine learning applications for data science, computer vision, and markov decision processes. I previously interned at UL Solutions as a Data Scientist, developing predictive models for phyisical flame tests. This summer I am interning at Evernorth Health, a subsidiary of The Cigna Group as a Data Science intern working within their PBM group. Additionally I have worked on ML modelling projects related to Human Position Estimation, Object Detection as well as Financial Portfolio Optimization.

Outside of my academic interest, I am a competitive (mid-distance) runner and have been involved in varsity athletics throughout all my years of college. I also am an avid trumpet player and play in community bands during the summer months. Feel free to reach out to me by email if you have any questions or inquiries.
